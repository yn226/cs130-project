Our History

The Roasted Bean was first opened in Chicago in 1999, with 3 new locations opening since then.

We pride ourselves on our perfectly roasted coffee beans, sourced ethically from around the world.

Why visit The Roasted Bean?

We offer a unique environment for focus and collaboration. So whether you just want to grab drinks with a friend, energize for a study session, or get some group work done, we are the perfect place for you!
